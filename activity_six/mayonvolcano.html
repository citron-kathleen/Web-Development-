Mayon Volcano



About the Mayon Volcano:

The name "Mayon," derived from the Bicolano word "magayon," meaning beautiful, perfectly describes this natural wonder. Beyond its beauty, the volcano holds centuries of history and folklore, making it a symbol of resilience and culture in the region. Rising 2,462 meters above sea level, its majestic presence dominates the Albay province skyline, offering breathtaking views from nearly every corner of the region.
