The Mind Museum



About the Mind Museum:

The Mind Museum is the first world-class, large-scale science museum in the Philippines, located in Bonifacio Global City, Taguig. It features over 250 interactive exhibits and five interconnected galleries—Atom, Life, Earth, Universe, and Technology—that explore the wonders of science for all ages. The museum's mission is to engage people in science, elevate community and national life, and inspire solutions to real-world problems through its exhibits, planetarium shows, and educational programs.
