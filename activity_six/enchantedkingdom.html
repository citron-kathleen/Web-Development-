Enchanted Kingdom



About the Enchanted Kingdom:

Enchanted Kingdom is the first and only world-class theme park in the Philippines, located in Santa Rosa, Laguna, and offers various themed zones, thrill rides, and live attractions. Managed by Enchanted Kingdom Inc., the park aims to create lasting magical experiences for families and thrill-seekers, with attractions such as the Space Shuttle, EKstreme Tower, and the flying theater, Agila the EKsperience: SARIBUHAY.
